<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The GPON Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Line Terminal (OLT)</li><li>Optical Network Terminal (ONT)</li><li>Passive Optical Splitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPON equipment market primarily consists of three key components. The Optical Line Terminal (OLT) is situated at the service provider's central office, managing data flow to and from subscribers. The Optical Network Terminal (ONT) is installed at the user's premises, converting optical signals to electrical signals for end-user devices. Passive Optical Splitters distribute the optical signal from the OLT to multiple ONTs, enabling efficient bandwidth sharing and reducing deployment costs in fiber-to-the-home (FTTH) networks.</p></p>
