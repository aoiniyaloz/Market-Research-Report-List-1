<p><ul><li>Axial Standard Recovery Rectifier</li><li>SMD Standard Recovery Rectifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Standard Recovery Rectifier market is primarily categorized into two types: Axial Standard Recovery Rectifiers and SMD Standard Recovery Rectifiers. Axial rectifiers feature leads for through-hole mounting, making them ideal for traditional circuit boards requiring robust connections. In contrast, SMD (Surface Mount Device) rectifiers are designed for surface mounting, offering compactness and efficiency in modern electronics. Both types are essential for converting AC to DC power in various applications, including power supplies, automotive electronics, and industrial equipment.</p></p>
