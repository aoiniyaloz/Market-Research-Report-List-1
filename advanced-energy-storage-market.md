Battery storage utilizes chemical reactions to store and release energy quickly. Flywheel storage uses kinetic energy in rotating disks for rapid energy release and high efficiency. Other technologies include compressed air and supercapacitors, each offering unique benefits for grid stability and renewable energy integration.</p></p>
