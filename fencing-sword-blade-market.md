<p><p>The fencing sword blade market is categorized into three primary types: foil, epee, and sabre. The foil is a lightweight, flexible weapon focused on point-based scoring, primarily targeting the torso. The epee, heavier and sturdier, allows for scoring anywhere on the body, emphasizing strategy and timing. The sabre, characterized by its curved blade, is designed for slashing attacks and targets the upper body, with a greater emphasis on speed and aggression. Each type caters to different styles and preferences in the sport.</p></p>
