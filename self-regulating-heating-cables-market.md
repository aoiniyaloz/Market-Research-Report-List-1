<p><p>Self-regulating heating cables are categorized into three main types: CABT (Constant Ambient Temperature), CAMT (Constant Ambient Medium Temperature), and CAHT (Constant Ambient High Temperature). CABT cables are designed to maintain a stable temperature in moderate environments, while CAMT cables are optimized for medium-temperature applications, providing versatility in various conditions. CAHT cables, on the other hand, cater to high-temperature requirements, ensuring safe and efficient heating in demanding environments. Each type is tailored to specific applications, enhancing energy efficiency and operational reliability.</p></p>
