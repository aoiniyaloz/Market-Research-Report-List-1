<p><p>The photovoltaic roof tiles market comprises mainly two types: monocrystalline and polycrystalline. Monocrystalline tiles are made from single crystal silicon, offering higher efficiency and a sleek aesthetic, making them ideal for residential applications. In contrast, polycrystalline tiles are composed of multiple silicon crystals, typically providing a lower efficiency but at a more affordable price point. Both types cater to the growing demand for sustainable energy solutions in the construction sector, appealing to environmentally conscious homeowners and builders.</p></p>
