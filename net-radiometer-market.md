<p><p>The net radiometer market is primarily divided into two types: 2-component net radiometers and 4-component net radiometers. </p><p>2-component net radiometers measure incoming and outgoing solar radiation, focusing on net radiation balance. They are widely used in agricultural and meteorological applications. </p><p>In contrast, 4-component net radiometers evaluate four components: shortwave and longwave radiation, allowing for a detailed assessment of energy exchange and surface interactions. This type is commonly utilized in climate research and environmental monitoring for comprehensive data collection.</p></p>
